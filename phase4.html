Phase 4: Pre-Launch Preparation

Mission Task

This is the final stage of pre-launch. You are now responsible for ensuring all systems are ready and stable before initiating the rocket launch.

Steps to Prepare for Launch in KSP

  • Step 1: In the **Space Center**, click on the **Launch Pad** option to load the rocket that has been prepared by the engineers.
  • Step 2: On the **Launch Pad**, ensure that all stages are correctly set up. Check that the decouplers, boosters, and payloads are aligned in the staging menu.
  • Step 3: Set the **Throttle** to 100% by pressing **Z**.
  • Step 4: Activate **SAS** (Stability Assist System) by pressing **T**. This will ensure your rocket stays steady during ascent.
  • Step 5: Make sure the rocket is pointing straight up (check the **Navball** at the bottom of the screen).
  • Step 6:** Confirm that fuel, communication, and all systems are "go" for launch.

Final Check

  • All systems are ready, communication is active, and weather conditions are clear.
  • Before you Press **Spacebar** to ignite the engines and begin the launch, head over to the next section!
Once you're ready, move to the next phase for guidance on achieving Low Earth Orbit (LEO).
Proceed to Launch!
