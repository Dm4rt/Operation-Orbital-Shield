Space Operations - Operation Orbital Shield

Welcome to the Space Operations team. Your mission is to manage the satellite’s launch and orbital maneuvers using **Kerbal Space Program** (KSP). You must launch the satellite successfully, place it in the correct orbit, and avoid any adversary threats. Stay alert for intelligence inputs and cyber warnings that could affect your mission.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Monitor and launch the satellite payload using KSP.
  • Monitor the satellite’s position and perform orbital adjustments.
  • Respond to intelligence updates on potential adversary satellite interception.
  • Defend the satellite from electromagnetic warfare attacks and other threats.
