Acquisitions & Dev Engineers Hub

Welcome to the Acquisitions and Development Engineers track for Operation Orbital Shield. Your mission is to support the space operations team by selecting, managing, and assembling the right contracts to ensure the successful launch of a satellite into Low Earth Orbit (LEO).

You will work in phases, starting with the acquisition of necessary rocket parts and ending with a full rocket assembly ready for the launch team.
