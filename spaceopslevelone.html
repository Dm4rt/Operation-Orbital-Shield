Operation Radio Silence

Mission Brief

You are tasked with conducting covert operations to secure military communications. Enemy forces are using advanced electromagnetic warfare (EMW) tactics to disrupt communication lines. Your objective is to detect interference, counteract jamming, and maintain secure satellite communications.

Objectives

  1. Monitor the electromagnetic spectrum for jamming and interference.
  2. Hop between frequencies to avoid detection and ensure continuous secure communication.
  3. Deploy counter-jamming measures and encryption to protect transmissions.
  4. Intercept enemy communications without revealing your position.
Current Objective: Monitor the spectrum for interference.
