Mission Brief: Operation Orbital Shield

In this mission, you are responsible for ensuring the successful launch and defense of a vital satellite tasked with communications and intelligence gathering. The satellite must be deployed into Low Earth Orbit (LEO), where it will be vulnerable to electromagnetic warfare (EMW) and adversarial threats.

Two primary tasks stand before you:

  1. Satellite Launch: Your first task is to design and launch the satellite into orbit using the appropriate rocket configuration. Ensure that the satellite reaches its correct orbital parameters and remains stable in orbit.
  2. Electromagnetic Warfare Defense: Once the satellite is in orbit, it will face potential EMW attacks. Your second task is to defend the satellite by tuning frequencies, encrypting communications, and countering jamming signals.
