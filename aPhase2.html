Phase 2: Rocket Assembly

Mission Task

As engineers, your task is to build the rocket in **Kerbal Space Program (KSP)** using the parts selected by the Acquisition Managers. Ensure the rocket can achieve the correct orbit and deploy the satellite.

Steps

  • Step 1: Using the selected contracts, begin the rocket assembly in **KSP**.
  • Step 2: Ensure the correct staging is set up for boosters, decouplers, and the payload deployment.
  • Step 3: Make sure the rocket has enough fuel and thrust to reach **Low Earth Orbit (LEO)**.
  • Step 4: Test the rocket to ensure it meets mission requirements, and make any necessary adjustments.

Important: You are responsible for ensuring that the rocket is stable and capable of launching the satellite successfully.

Once the rocket is built and ready, proceed to the next phase for final review and launch preparation.
