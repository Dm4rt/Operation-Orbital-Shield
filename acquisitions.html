Acquisitions & Dev Engineers - Operation Orbital Shield

As a member of the Acquisitions and Development Engineering team, your mission is to design and build a rocket capable of launching a satellite payload into orbit. Using **Kerbal Space Program (KSP)**, you will need to ensure the satellite's specifications are met and that the rocket design is adaptable to potential mission challenges.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Design and build a rocket in KSP with sufficient fuel and power to reach orbit.
  • Ensure that the satellite payload is securely attached and can be deployed once in orbit.
  • Adapt to mission injects, such as cyberattacks or engineering challenges, and adjust the design as needed.
