Phase 3: Final Review

Mission Task

The final phase is a review of your team's work. Both the Acquisitions Managers and Engineers need to finalize all aspects of the mission to ensure everything is ready for the rocket launch.

Steps

  • Step 1: Review the selected contracts and ensure that the budget has not been exceeded.
  • Step 2: Confirm the rocket design meets mission requirements, including fuel capacity, thrust, and payload configuration.
  • Step 3: Conduct a final systems check to ensure that all components (fuel, engines, decouplers, etc.) are functional.
  • Step 4: If any changes need to be made to the design or budget, adjust them and recheck all systems.

Important: Once everything is finalized, you are ready to move the rocket to the launch phase for the Space Operations team to initiate the launch process.

Once your review is complete, proceed to hand over the rocket to Space Operations for launch.
