Intel Puzzle - Level 1

You have intercepted satellite images and intelligence reports from the adversary’s recent launches. Using OSINT techniques, analyze the images and data to identify key details about the adversary’s launch center, rocket, satellite constellation, and timing. Once you gather the correct information, you will unlock the next phase of intelligence and be able to intercept the adversary's transmission.

Questions:

1. What is the name of the launch center?

2. What is the name of the rocket launched?

3. When was the rocket launched?

4. How many remote-sensing satellites were launched aboard the rocket?

5. What constellation are the satellites a part of?
