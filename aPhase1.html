Phase 1: Contract Acquisition

Mission Task

As the Acquisition Managers, your role is to review, select, and manage contracts for rocket parts to support the satellite mission. You have a limited budget and must ensure the selected contracts will allow the engineers to build a rocket capable of reaching Low Earth Orbit (LEO).

Available Contracts: You can review the available contracts by clicking here.

Steps

  • Step 1: Review the available contracts for engines, fuel tanks, decouplers, and payload adapters.
  • Step 2: Each contract has specific **costs** and **performance ratings** (e.g., thrust, fuel efficiency). Select parts that fit within your **budget**.
  • Step 3: Ensure the selected parts will allow for the successful launch and deployment of the satellite.
  • Step 4: Submit your final **budget sheet** and parts selection for review by the engineers.

Important: You must balance performance and cost to stay within budget while ensuring the rocket can achieve the mission objectives.
