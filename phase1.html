Phase 1: Satellite Command and Control

Mission Task

Your task in this phase is to plan the command and control (C2) system for the satellite. This includes communication with ground stations, encryption, and secure transmission plans.

  • Determine the primary ground stations for satellite communication.
  • Develop a secure communication plan, including encryption protocols.
  • Plan contingency actions in case of signal loss or interference.

Remember: Effective satellite command and control ensures the mission's success, even in hostile environments.

Once the plan is ready, proceed to the next phase.
