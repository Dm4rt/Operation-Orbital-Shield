Acquisitions & Dev Engineers - Level 1

Your task in this level is to successfully design a rocket in **Kerbal Space Program (KSP)** that can carry a satellite payload into orbit. Follow the steps below and use the blueprint images to guide you through the process.

Step 1: Open **KSP** and start a new rocket design in the **Vehicle Assembly Building (VAB)**. Begin by choosing a suitable **command module** and ensure you have space for the satellite payload.

Step 2: Attach **fuel tanks** and **engines** to your command module. Balance fuel efficiency and power for a successful launch into orbit.

Step 3: Use **decouplers** between each stage to ensure you can separate rocket stages once they’re no longer needed. Attach the satellite payload to the upper stage with a decoupler.

Step 4: Ensure the satellite is attached securely and can be deployed once in orbit. You may need to use **solar panels** and **RCS thrusters** for the satellite to maneuver once it’s deployed.
